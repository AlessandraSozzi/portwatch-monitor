This page presents an experimental nowcasting model of global maritime trade, leveraging real-time satellite data on vessel movements. The model aims to provide a timely indicator of global merchandise trade. Exports and imports are both valued on a free-on-board (fob) basis. The estimates are updated on a monthly basis with a lag of 7 working days and are subject to revision. For background on the methodology, please see here. For limitations of the data and approach, please see here. We welcome your feedback at IMF-PortWatch@imf.org.
Source: IMF PortWatch (portwatch.imf.org).
